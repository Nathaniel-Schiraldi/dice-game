Dice Rolling Game Description:

You will be asked to choose a username with only letters, a length of 4 to 8 characters, and it must be in ascending order.

You will be asked to choose a difficulty level, either easy, medium, or hard.

You will be asked to roll two dices! Their values will be added to form a total. Your selection of difficulty determines your destiny of winning or losing!

However, there is one saving grace. If you manage to guess the exact total produced from the two dice you will win automatically. This rule supersedes all other difficulty rules.

Easy: 6-12 (inclusive) = Win // 2-5 (inclusive) = Defeat

Medium: 8-12 (inclusive) = Win // 2-7 (inclusive) = Defeat

Hard: 10-12 (inclusive) = Win // 2-9 (inclusive) = Defeat

If User Prediction = Total ===> Undisputed Win!!!
